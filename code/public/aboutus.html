About SkillSwap

SkillSwap is a platform designed to connect people who want to learn and share skills. Whether you're a graphic designer, a cook, a programmer, or a musician, SkillSwap helps you find others in the community who can teach you something new or benefit from what you know.

This project is built using Firebase for authentication, database and hosting, and Tailwind CSS for a sleek and responsive design. It's created by passionate developers aiming to make learning and sharing knowledge easier and more accessible.

We're constantly improving, and your feedback helps us grow. Let's build a community where everyone can teach and learn — together.
